au Gratin Potatpes

These cheesy potatoes will surely be a hit

INGREDIENTS
  • (4) Yukon gold potatoes, sliced in to 1/4 inch disks
  • (4)oz Extra sharp cheddar cheese, grated
  • (1) Small onion, sliced
  • (2) cups Heavy Cream
  • (3) Tbsp Butter
  • (3) Tbsp Flour
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
DIRECTIONS
  1. Start by preheating oven to 400 Degrees F
  2. Grease ovensafe baking pan and add one layer of sliced Potatoes
  3. Melt butter in pan over medium low heat
  4. Add Flour to melted Butter and mix together well, until Flour is browned
  5. Slowly stir in Heavy Cream
  6. Add shredded cheese while stiring constantly, Salt and Pepper to taste
  7. Once chesse is melted, pour over Potatoes, just enough to cover
  8. Add another layer of Potates on top and repeat previous step until Potatoes and Cheese mixture are used up
  9. Bake in 400 Degree F oven for 1 hour
This is time consuming but so worth it! Goes well with Elk Steak
