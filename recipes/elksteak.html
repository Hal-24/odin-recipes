Elk Steak

This recipe will guide you to mouth watering pan seared elk steak that can be enjoyed any night of the week

INGREDIENTS
  • 1 lb Elk backstrap
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1 clove garlic
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
DIRECTIONS
  1. Leave backstrap whole and rub with Salt & Pepper to taste
  2. Heat up cast iron on stovetop to medium-high heat and melt butter in pan
  3. Crush garlic and add to melted butter
  4. Sear backstrap on both sides, then turn heat down to medium
  5. Cook backstrap until interal temperature is 135 Degrees F
  6. Remove backstrap from pan and let rest before slicing to desired steak thickness

This recipe goes well with au Gratin Potatoes or leftovers used in Steak Sandwichesclear
